FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before our contact

How much does a Product Animation cost ? +
Pricing depends on the creative scope and complexity of each project, usually ranging from $100 to $3000. I highly recommend sending me an e-mail with your request and I'll give you a quote within 24 hours.
How long will it take to receive my product ? +
Delivery time typically ranges from 3 to 14 days, depending on the project’s complexity and creative scope.
Do I need to send you a physical sample of my product ? +
No. High-resolution images of the product and the brand label files are usually enough. If you already have existing assets or product files available online, the production process can be faster and more cost-effective.
How do revisions work ? +
Each project includes 1 Free Revision. Revisions are typically completed within a few days and sent back for approval. Additional revisions can also be requested and will be billed based on the hours required.
What payment methods do you accept? +
I typically work with bank transfers, PayPal, or Payoneer. For international clients, we can discuss the most efficient platform for secure transactions.
Can you provide raw project files? +
By default, I deliver high-quality final exports (MP4, JPG, PNG). However, if you require the source 3D project files for internal use, this can be negotiated for an additional license fee.
How can we start ? +
Send me an e-mail explaining your request and I'll answer as fast as possible.
